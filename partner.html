Frequently Asked Questions

How much can I earn?

Earnings vary based on location and space. Our partners typically earn $500-$3,000 per month. You set your own prices!

Is my business insured?

Yes! We provide $10,000 protection for every bag stored, covering both your business and the customer's belongings.

How do I get paid?

Payments are processed automatically and deposited directly to your bank account every week. You keep 80% of the booking fee.

What requirements do I need?

A clean, secure space, regular business hours, and willingness to help travelers. No special equipment needed!
